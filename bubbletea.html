What is it?

Bubble tea is a cold and fun drink that is made with iced tea, either green or black tea, sweetened milk or flavouring, and tapioca or other toppings. It consists of sweetened tea with milk or flavourings.

What is the background knowledge?

The first bubble tea origins from the Hanlin teahouse in Tainan, Taiwan in 1986. The owner: Tu Twong-he got the inspiration of making bubble tea by coming upon white tapioca balls. He made tea with the traditional white tapioca balls and since it had the appearance of pearls, it created the 'pearl tea'. Not long after, Hanlin modified the white tapioca pearls to the black tapioca pearls we use today. Another origin is the Chun Shui Tang Teahouse in Taichung, Taiwan . During a visit to Japan in the 1980s, the founder Liu Han-Chieh took inspiration from the cold coffee being served and decided to apply this method to tea. However, it was Lin Hsiu Hui that created bubble tea. He poured a sweetened pudding with tapioca balls into the iced tea drink in 1988. During 1990s, it became really popular in most areas in East and Southeast Asia. Further on, it became popular among global consumers.
